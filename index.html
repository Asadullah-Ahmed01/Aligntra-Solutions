FAQs
Common questions

If your question isn't here, reach out — we're happy to talk.

What types of engineering roles do you fill?

Frontend, backend, full-stack, DevOps, data engineering, ML, mobile, QA, and security. We focus exclusively on technical roles across all seniority levels.

How do you vet your engineers?

Every candidate goes through a technical screening, a portfolio or work-history review, and a communication assessment. We only put forward engineers we'd hire ourselves.

What does it cost to work with Aligntra?

For clients, we work on a success-based model — you only pay when you hire someone through us. Engineers pay nothing. Reach out and we'll walk you through the specifics.

I'm an engineer — how long until I hear back?

We review every submission within 3 business days. If your profile matches an open role, we'll reach out to schedule a quick intro call. Otherwise, you'll stay in our network for future matches.

Do you support remote and international placements?

Yes. We place engineers across 12 countries and work with fully remote, hybrid, and on-site setups. We handle timezone coordination and compliance considerations.

What happens if a placement doesn't work out?

We offer a replacement guarantee. If a hire doesn't work out within the guarantee period, we'll find a replacement at no additional cost.
