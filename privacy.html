Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Aligntra Solutions ("we", "us", "our") is a recruitment agency that connects engineers with companies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly through our forms:

  • Client enquiry form: name, company name, work email, phone number, and a description of roles you're hiring for.
  • Engineer application form: first name, last name, email, phone number, primary skill, years of experience, CV/resume file, and any additional notes you choose to include.

We also collect basic usage data through cookies (see Section 5).

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to client hiring enquiries and match them with suitable engineers.
  • Review engineer applications and match them with open roles at our client companies.
  • Communicate with you about opportunities, updates, and our services.
  • Improve our website and recruitment process.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your profile with prospective employers (for engineers) or candidate profiles with your company (for clients), but only as part of the recruitment process you have opted into.

3. CV and file storage

CVs uploaded through our website are stored securely in private cloud storage. They are not publicly accessible and can only be accessed by authorised members of the Aligntra team. We retain CVs for as long as they are relevant to active recruitment, and you may request deletion at any time.

4. Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. If you'd like us to delete your data, contact us at the email below and we'll process your request within 30 days.

5. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preference.
  • Support basic rate limiting to prevent form abuse.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics. When you first visit our site, you'll be asked to accept or decline cookies. Your choice is stored locally and respected on future visits.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

7. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted storage, access controls, and secure transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, contact us at privacy@aligntra.com.
